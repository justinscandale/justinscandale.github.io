ADMINISTRATIVE REPORTS
Student Counts (Funding)
This report displays the headcount, including demographics, of individual students taking courses funded by a particular campus. If a student takes courses funded by multiple campuses, they are counted at each campus where they took a course. So, duplication exists if campuses are added together. However, Total University headcount is not duplicated.
Student Counts (Physical)
This report displays the headcount, including demographics, of individual students taking courses physically located at a particular campus. If a student takes courses physically located at multiple campuses, they are counted at each campus where they took a course. So, duplication exists if campuses are added together. However, Total University headcount is not duplicated.
Student Headcount (CIP)
This report displays USFs enrolled student headcount by CIP (Classification of Instructional Programs).
Student Credit Hours (CIP)
This report displays student credit hours by CIP (Classification of Instructional Programs).
Degrees Awarded (CIP)
This report displays Degrees Awarded by CIP (Classification of Instructional Programs).
Average GPA
This report provides average cumulative GPA and average Term GPA for all students by student level.
Room Utilization
This report displays a matrix of how many rooms are being used by hour of the day and day of the week and detailed information about when and where courses are meeting.
Section Count by Size Range
This report displays the number of course sections taught within size ranges. Data are displayed for all courses or for a selected course type. Include remote learning courses or report them separately.
Grade Distribution
This report displays the distribution of grades at course level, including number and percentage within each grade.
Course Demand Report (Enrollments)
This report lists the most popular courses (by student heads) taken by students using the students homecampus, college, department and major as filters.
Course Demand Report (Funding)
This report lists the most popular courses (by student heads) taken by students using the funding campus, division & department of the course as filters.
Course Load Matrix
Whos taking your courses? This report displays the instances of enrollment and the credit hours generated by all types of students (students in the department of the course, students in the same college as the course, etc.) taking courses sorted by selected campus, college and departments.
